About KhojAI

Revolutionizing Missing Persons Identification with Artificial Intelligence

Our Mission

KhojAI is dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge AI technology to help reunite missing persons with their loved ones.

Every year, thousands of people go missing worldwide, leaving families in distress. Traditional search methods can be time-consuming and often lack the necessary resources. KhojAI aims to change that by providing law enforcement and families with powerful tools to identify missing individuals quickly and accurately.

Helping families reunite through technology

How It Works

Our advanced AI technology makes the search process efficient and effective

1. Upload

Upload a clear photo of the missing person along with relevant details to create a searchable profile.

2. Search

Our AI analyzes facial features and matches them against our database of missing persons.

3. Get Notified

Receive instant notifications when potential matches are found, helping you reconnect with your loved ones faster.

Our Technology

KhojAI utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning models for facial recognition and pattern matching. Our system is designed to work with various image qualities and can identify individuals even after significant time has passed.
